Please revise the homepage narrative of the website.

Important change:
The homepage should no longer be structured mainly around service categories.
Instead, it should be structured around the 4 AI agents that power the product.

Old direction:
The site explained the business from a service perspective.

New direction:
The site should explain the business from an agent perspective.

Core concept:
This is not just a restaurant service business.
It is an AI-agent-powered restaurant growth platform.

The homepage should present the product as an AI team for restaurant growth, where different agents are responsible for different parts of the business.

The 4 agents are:
- Operation Agent
- Chef Agent
- Social Media Agent
- Customer Service Agent

What each agent does:

Operation Agent
- business growth overview
- delivery performance
- budget recommendations
- ad spend efficiency
- platform promotion strategy

Chef Agent
- new dish recommendations
- seasonal menu ideas
- menu image optimization
- dish description optimization

Social Media Agent
- posting performance
- engagement insights
- content suggestions
- influencer / KOL / KOC collaboration

Customer Service Agent
- Google Maps and Yelp review monitoring
- delivery platform complaints and follow-up
- customer feedback insights
- repeat customer / win-back recommendations

Homepage direction:
Present the company as an AI team, not a list of disconnected services.

The homepage should communicate:
- restaurant owners get an AI team, not just agency-style services
- the agents work together inside one shared Boardroom
- the owner interacts with the system through chat, insights, and approvals
- AI handles the day-to-day work across growth, menu, content, reputation, and retention

Suggested homepage structure:
1. Hero section
2. Meet Your AI Team section
3. Boardroom / how the agents work together
4. What AI handles vs what the owner controls
5. Business outcomes / value created
6. CTA section

Suggested messaging direction:
- Four AI agents. One Boardroom. One growth system for restaurants.
- Your restaurant growth team, powered by AI agents.
- Run restaurant growth with AI agents, not more manual work.

For the “Meet Your AI Team” section, create 4 agent cards with clear descriptions.
Keep the content clear, commercial, and operator-friendly.

Important:
The homepage should now feel more AI-native, more differentiated, and more aligned with the product experience.
